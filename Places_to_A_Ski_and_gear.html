What Gear do I Need to Alpine Ski?

This is the gear you will need to Alpine Ski:

  • Ski boots (used to bind the ski to the skiers foot.)
  • Skis
  • Helmet
  • Goggles
  • Ski Poles (Optional
  • Ski Pants
  • Gloves/Mittens
  • Clothing to keep your body warm

Where Should You Ski?

Being home to almost 300 ski resorts, Canada is the perfect place to start skiing! Many ski resorts are home to ski schools, with over 20 thousand Canadian Ski Instructors' Alliance certified teachers you can choose to ski recreationally or competitively!
